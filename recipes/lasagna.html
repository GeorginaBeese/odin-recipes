Lasagne Recipe

Description

This is a Lasagne recipie

Ingredients

  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Lasagne Sheets
  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • Tinned tomatos
  1. Chop the onions, garlic, carrots and celery.
  2. Over medium-low heat, cook the onions for 5 minutes, then turn down to low and cook for a further 15 minutes.
  3. Heat the oven to 170℃/150℃ Fan/325°F.
  4. Bake the lasagna for 45 Minutes.
