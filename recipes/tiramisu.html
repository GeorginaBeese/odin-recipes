Tiramisu Recipe

Description

This is a Tiramisu recipie

Ingredients

  • Mascapone
  • Lady Fingers
  • Lasagne Sheets
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Espresso
  1. Wisk the egg whites and the egg yolks seperatly.
  2. Stir in the sugar and mascapone.
  3. Soak the lady fingers in the espresso.
  4. Assemble the layers.
